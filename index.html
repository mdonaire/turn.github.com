Giving back to the open-source community

Turn relies heavily on field-proven, open-source software in all the layers of our technology stack. From Linux-based servers to the many open-source Java libraries our software uses, open-source software plays a crucial role in Turn's success. This is why Turn nows gives back to the community by making public some of its in-house developments under free and open-source software licences.

Turn chose Github has the hosting platform for its open-source projects. Visit github.com/turn for a list of our public repositories.
